Who Are We

The introduction of the automobile idea into our lives goes back even further than you might imagine. With the first engine trials, which started with the use of steam energy in the 1700s, trials began for the use of wheeled vehicles as a means of transport. These vehicles, which are incomparably slow and cumbersome, started to break speed records in a short time as a result of the discovery of oil and the advancement of technology with gigantic steps.
